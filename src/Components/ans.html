Online Aptitude Test - ProSIM
Thank you for applying to ProSIM!!

As the first step, please take up this online aptitude test. The test consists of the following engineering sections:
1. Strength of materials
2. Vibration
3. FEM
4. Heat Transfer
5. Engineering Design
6. Fluid Mechanics
7. Drawing reading

Total no of questions = 70 (all multiple choice questions)
Total no of points = 70 (1 point per question)
Total time for test 60 mins

Based on your aptitude test score, you will be taken to the next stages of training & internship.

ProSIM is an design and CAE/FEA engineering services provider to a vast domain of sectors such as Nuclear, Oil & Gas, thermal power, energy, defence, aerospace, heavy engineering, power engineering, railways and general engineering. ProSIM has gathered over 20+ years of expertise in CAE & FEA and is amongst the leading engineering industries for Nuclear energy in India.

ProSIM uses variety of CAE & FEA and engineering tools, develops in-house macros / subroutines/ programs /and simulation protocols to deliver high value to its customers.
